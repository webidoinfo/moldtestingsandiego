Our Mold Investigation Process

1. Initial Assessment

We begin by locating the source of moisture and identifying hidden water damage. Our experts use a science-based approach to focus our mold investigation in the right areas.

2. Comprehensive Testing

Using advanced equipment, we collect air samples and analyze building materials to determine the extent of mold exposure. We also take an outdoor sample for comparison.

3. Detailed Analysis

Our lab provides clear results and identifies types of mold present. We then develop a comprehensive report with our findings and recommendations.

Protecting Your Health

Mold exposure can pose serious health risks, especially if you or a family member has health concerns like asthma or allergies. If you're allergic to mold, it's crucial to address any mold issue promptly.

Our thorough mold investigations help identify potential health hazards and provide the information needed to restore your office to a healthy state.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need mold testing?

If you notice musty odors, visible mold growth, recent water damage, or if you're experiencing unexplained health issues, professional mold testing can identify hidden mold and assess air quality.

What does a mold inspection include?

Our comprehensive mold inspections include visual assessment, air quality testing, moisture readings, thermal imaging, and surface sampling when necessary. We provide detailed reports with lab results and recommendations.

How long does a mold inspection take?

A typical inspection takes 1-2 hours depending on the property size. Lab results are usually available within 2-3 business days.

Do you also perform mold remediation?

No, we focus solely on mold testing and inspection to avoid any conflict of interest. We can recommend trusted remediation companies if needed.

Are your mold inspectors certified?

Yes, all our mold inspectors are certified professionals with extensive training in mold detection, air quality testing, and building science.
